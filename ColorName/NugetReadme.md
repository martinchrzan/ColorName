# ColorName
## Get a color name from a provided HEX or RGB 


**Usage:**

    ColorNameProvider.GetColorNameFromRGB(123,45,1);
    ColorNameProvider.GetColorNameFromHex("#ABABAB");
    ColorNameProvider.GetColorNameFromHex("ABABAB");
    ColorNameProvider.GetColorNameFromHex("#FFF");
    ColorNameProvider.GetColorNameFromHex("FFF");
